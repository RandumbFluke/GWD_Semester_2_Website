A little about us

Services we provide as well as some information on how we came to be.

Hello, we’re Simon and Minty together we are the owners of Hyper Fragrance Revolution, an arcade retro style laundromat. As ex-students and young working professionals we know how boring and inconvenient the laundry is, it can be such a drag so, we thought why not spice it up.

We pride ourselves on our multitude of services such as wash and fold services when hiring staff, a self-service if you like to DIY, an ATM on site, free internet and tv – showing all the latest channels (just ask a member of staff for a specific channel, first come first serve though) as well as many more.

Cafe and Open Mic Nights

Are you interested in performing? Everyone is welcome!

Are you a performer or someone who likes to be a star? Well in our laundromat every Saturday we hold an open mic night, with thirty-minute slots all performers are welcome. Just talk to a member of staff about available time slots and they will sort it out. Our café runs a bar after 6pm (which is when performances begin).
At the end of the night, we hold a vote on best/most enjoyable performance and the winner gets a ticket for free washing, drying and folding all done by a member of staff.
